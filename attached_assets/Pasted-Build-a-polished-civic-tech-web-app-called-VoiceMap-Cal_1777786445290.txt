Build a polished civic-tech web app called “VoiceMap California: Animal Welfare Evidence Hub.”

Purpose:
A statewide California public-interest tool that helps residents document reported concerns involving stray cats, stray dogs, animal neglect, and animal welfare system failures. The app should organize reports by city, county, issue type, and urgency, then generate responsible reports for local agencies, state officials, lawmakers, and media.

Important legal/safety framing:
Use careful wording:
- “reported concern”
- “alleged concern”
- “submitted evidence”
- “requires review”
- “requires investigation”
- “publicly submitted animal welfare concern”
- “pattern of reported concerns”
Do NOT declare abuse occurred. Do NOT identify anyone as guilty. Do NOT encourage harassment or confrontation.

Design style:
Use a premium MacOS Tahoe / Apple Liquid Glass inspired design:
- glassmorphism interface
- translucent frosted panels
- glowing animated borders
- luminous moving background orbs
- dark navy / cyan / aqua / white glass aesthetic
- subtle California gold accents
- clean infographic / visual roadmap structure
- bold title and subtitle
- color-coded sections
- rounded cards
- professional public-interest feel
- modern, clean, emotional but credible

Tech:
React + Vite + Tailwind CSS.
Use localStorage for version 1.
No paid APIs.
No backend required, but create clean structure for future Supabase integration.

Responsible Reporting Page:
Include clear warnings:
- Do not trespass
- Do not harass property owners
- Do not post private addresses publicly without care
- Do not make claims you cannot support
- Contact emergency services for immediate danger
- This tool helps organize concerns; it does not determine legal guilt.
